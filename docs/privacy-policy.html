Privacy Policy for PersonaSphere

Last updated: April 18, 2026

PersonaSphere is an offline personality quiz app. We designed the app to work without requiring account signup and without collecting personal data for analytics or advertising.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or sell personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, or payment information.

2. How the App Uses Data

Quiz progress, preferences, and favorites are saved locally on your device so you can continue using the app between sessions. This local data is not sent to our servers.

3. Internet and Network Access

The core features of PersonaSphere work offline. The app does not require an account and does not use third-party analytics SDKs for tracking user behavior.

4. Sharing Content

If you choose to share quiz results, PersonaSphere opens your device's system share sheet and only shares the content you select. We do not receive copies of what you share.

5. Device Permissions

The app may request vibration permission on Android to provide optional haptic feedback. No sensitive permissions such as contacts, microphone, or location are required for normal app use.

6. Children

PersonaSphere does not knowingly collect personal data from children or any users.

7. Data Deletion

Since data is stored locally on your device, you can clear app data from your device settings or uninstall the app to remove stored information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@personasphere.app
